    As in 2020 the UET as changed its aggregate formula for the admissions. This is a great opportunity for the students to get admission.
    The New Aggregate formula will be the sum of 25%(percent) marks of Matriculation , 45% (percent) marks of Intermediate Part-1 and 30% marks of UET's Entry Test (ECAT).


    The Calculation of aggregate is done by the formula:

    (Marks scored in Matric × 25 /Total Marks of Matric) + (Marks scored in intermediate Part-1 × 45/Total Marks of Part-1) + (Marks scored in entry test × 30/400) = Aggregate.
